🏺 Ancient: Katapayadi System

What is Katapayadi?

An ancient Indian system that converts Sanskrit/Malayalam letters to numbers, used for encoding astronomical data, dates, and secret messages in verses and literature.

What is AES?

Advanced Encryption Standard - a symmetric encryption algorithm used worldwide for securing data. It uses complex mathematical operations and is virtually unbreakable with current technology.

🏛️ Historical Techniques

  • Invisible Ink: Lemon juice, heat-revealed messages
  • Microdots: Photographs reduced to period-size
  • Null Ciphers: Hidden in innocent-looking letters
  • Katapayadi Poetry: Numbers disguised as verses

💻 Digital Methods

  • LSB Substitution: Modify least significant bits in images
  • Audio Steganography: Hide data in sound frequencies
  • Network Steganography: Covert channels in protocols
  • Blockchain Steganography: Hidden messages in transactions
